About Me

I am a web developer and SEO specialist based in Devon, helping small businesses get online with simple and affordable websites.

Hey, I'm Zara!

I live and work in the beautiful county of Devon in the South West UK. I have 7 years' experience in the web industry. Having previously worked for a web development agency in Somerset for 5 years, I took the plunge into freelance work in 2022.
