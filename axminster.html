How It Works

I will work closely with you throughout the website creation process, from the initial discussion, through to the launch of the site and supporting you after the launch. Each website build is different depending on the needs of each client and business, but most roughly follow the below process.

Initial Meeting

In this digital age, location is never an issue. We can meet on Zoom or chat over the phone, or if you're local to Axminster, we could meet for a coffee. We will discuss your business, get to know each other and have a chat about your budget and expectations. I can then work out a quote for you and send you a full proposal for you to review.

Design and Branding

Once we've agreed a budget, signed a contract and pressed 'go' on your project, we can get to work. I can start creating the website using your existing branding, or we can work together to agree a colour scheme, font and style that best reflect your business and industry. I can recommend a graphic designer to create a logo if needed.

Creating Your Website

Once you are happy with your branding, I can start creating the website. Once the first draft of the Homepage is complete, I will share it with you to ensure you're happy with the general look and feel. I can then crack on with creating the rest fo your website pages. I will update you on progress at various points, agreed in advance.

Time for Feedback

Once the first draft of the website is ready, I will share it with you and eagerly await your feedback. You will have two rounds of amends included in the process. Once I have received your feedback, I can make the suggested amends and share the final result with you.

Testing

As people predominantly search the internet on mobile devices, it's important to ensure your website looks great at every size. I will thoroughly test your website on a range of different devices, including desktop, tablet and mobile, to ensure it looks beautiful and is easy to use no matter the screen size.

Add Content and Launch

Your final draft of written content and images will be added to the website. I will provide a 1-hour training session to ensure you can use your new website and add content yourself if you'd like to. I will then do some final tweaks and checks, and when we're both happy, I will press the 'Launch' button!
