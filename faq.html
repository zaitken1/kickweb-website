Search Engine Optimisation

SEO (Search Engine Optimization) is the process of improving and optimising your website so that search engines like Google and Bing can easily find and understand it. The better your SEO, the higher your site is likely to rank when people search for your products or services. Without SEO, your website might look great but struggle to attract the right customers, or any customers at all.

SEO isn't a quick fix. It’s more like planting seeds than flipping a switch. Most websites start to see improvements within 6 months, depending on how competitive your industry is and how much work is put into content and optimisation, but I recommend at least 12 months of SEO work to really see results. Big results usually come with consistent effort over time. The good news is that once you start climbing the rankings, the benefits can really snowball. However, it's worth mentioning that SEO results are never guaranteed. All we can do is optimise your website for search engines to give you the best chance of ranking and getting in front of your ideal client.
